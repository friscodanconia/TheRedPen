The Red Pen
Privacy Policy

The Red Pen does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

What we collect

Nothing. Zero. The Red Pen processes all text entirely within your browser using local JavaScript. Your writing never leaves your device.

What we don't do

  • No data is collected, stored, or sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking scripts of any kind
  • No cookies or local storage used for tracking
  • No third-party services or SDKs
  • No accounts, sign-ups, or authentication required
  • No ads, no monetization of your data

How it works

The Red Pen runs a lightweight text analysis engine directly in your browser tab. When you type in a LinkedIn post editor, the extension reads the text from the DOM, analyzes writing patterns locally, and displays a score. The text is never copied, cached, or transmitted anywhere.

Permissions

The extension requests no special browser permissions beyond running on linkedin.com pages. It uses only a content script that activates on LinkedIn to detect the post editor and provide real-time feedback.

Open source

The Red Pen is fully open source. You can inspect every line of code to verify these privacy claims yourself.
